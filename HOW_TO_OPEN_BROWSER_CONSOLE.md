## Safari (Mac)

### First Time Setup:
1. **Enable Developer Menu:**
   - Open Safari
   - Click **Safari** ‚Üí **Settings** (or **Preferences**)
   - Go to **Advanced** tab
   - ‚úÖ Check **"Show Develop menu in menu bar"**

### Open Console:
1. Click **Develop** in the menu bar (top of screen)
2. Click **Show JavaScript Console** (or press `Cmd+Option+C`)
3. A window will appear at the bottom - that's the console!

## Chrome/Edge (Windows/Mac)

**Press:** `F12` or `Ctrl+Shift+I` (Windows) / `Cmd+Option+I` (Mac)

Or:
- Right-click anywhere ‚Üí **Inspect** ‚Üí **Console** tab

## Firefox (Windows/Mac)

**Press:** `F12` or `Ctrl+Shift+K` (Windows) / `Cmd+Option+K` (Mac)
